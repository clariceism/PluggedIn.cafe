What is PluggedIn.cafe?

PluggedIn.cafe helps you find coffee shops with electric sockets for charging your devices in BGC, Taguig.

How do you gather data on coffee shops?

We collect data from users and our own surveys to ensure accuracy.

Can I add cafes from my area?

Yes, contributions from all over the world are welcome! You can help adding cafes from your area to our database by following the steps on how to contribute on Github. Or send me the details (name of cafe, latitude & longtitude coordinates, no. of sockets, and if Wi-Fi is available to [email]
